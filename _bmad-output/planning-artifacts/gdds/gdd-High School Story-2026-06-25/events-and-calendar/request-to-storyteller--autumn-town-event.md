## Purpose

We need a stronger proposal for the MVP town event. The first working idea was an `Autumn Town Fair`, but the designer correctly rejected the flavor as too generic and not especially believable for autumn. Please propose a more seasonally grounded town event that fits the nostalgic high-school life-sim fantasy.

## Current GDD Context

MVP is a one-semester vertical slice across a 20-week semester. The event slate currently targets:

- School-wide event: `Class Integration Day`.
- Town event: open, needs your proposal.
- Science Club special event: open.
- Drama Club special event: open.
- Week 20 exam period.

The town event should contrast with school life and show that the protagonist has a life beyond campus. It should support relationships, mood, memories, and optional social choices without becoming a large production burden.

## Tone Targets

The game prioritizes:

1. Nostalgia.
2. Attachment.
3. Bittersweet reflection.

The town event should feel ordinary but emotionally charged: the kind of seasonal local moment that becomes memorable because of who the player spends it with, what they miss, or what small detail they notice.

## Design Constraints

- Season: autumn semester.
- Avoid generic summer-like `town fair` / `festyn` flavor unless transformed into something clearly autumnal and locally plausible.
- Fixed calendar event: it can be missed or poorly used.
- Scope: one MVP event, not a full festival system.
- Must work with the existing town/location scope: cafe, park, convenience store, and event-specific temporary space if needed.
- Should create meaningful choices about limited time and who to spend it with.
- Should support relationship progression, Mood, small purchases/gifts/consumables, and social discovery.
- Should not require combat, minigames, large economy, or a big bespoke map.
- Should not replace classmate story milestones; it is a calendar anchor and social opportunity.

## Existing Systems It Can Touch

- Time blocks: 15-minute activity structure.
- Energy, Stress, Mood.
- Money: weekly allowance plus optional purchases.
- Gifts/consumables.
- Preferences: food, music, media, free-time style, social preference.
- Social Media: NPC current-location clues for known classmates.
- Map: known locations and travel feasibility, no NPC presence.
- Relationships: repeatable interactions, hangouts, lunch/food moments, small story flags.
- Phone calendar: known global town event.

## Requested Output

Please provide 3-5 town event concepts suitable for autumn. For each concept include:

- Event name.
- Short fiction premise.
- Why it is plausibly autumnal.
- Gameplay function.
- Relationship opportunities.
- Mood / memory potential.
- Required MVP assets or locations.
- Scope risk.
- One example scene or moment.

Then recommend one concept as the strongest MVP choice and explain why it best fits `High School Story`.

## Evaluation Criteria

Prioritize concepts that:

- Feel specific rather than generic.
- Can be produced with restrained MVP scope.
- Create relationship choices naturally.
- Support bittersweet/nostalgic tone.
- Use autumn as more than visual decoration.
- Fit a small town near a school/campus.
