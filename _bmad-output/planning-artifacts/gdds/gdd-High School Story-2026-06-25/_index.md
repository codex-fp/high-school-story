## Companion Folders

Companions are grouped by design topic, not by document type. File names use the pattern `type--topic.md`, with explicit direction when a file requests or responds to another session.

| Topic | Folder | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Brief reconciliation | `brief-reconciliation/` | Brief correction handoff after relationship/identity decisions. |
| Lessons and academics | `lessons-and-academics/` | Lesson actions, homework, teacher questions, and classroom variants. |
| Identity and preferences | `identity-and-preferences/` | Favorites/preferences ideation and accepted recommendation package. |
| Events and calendar | `events-and-calendar/` | Town event exploration and Harvest Evening selection. |
| Classmates, cast, and arcs | `classmates-cast-and-arcs/` | MVP cast process, audits, accepted package, and 3-beat arc guidance. |
| Clubs and arc access | `clubs-and-arc-access/` | Drama-to-Literary Club revision and club gating decisions. |
