## Decision

Accepted: replace **Drama Club** with **Literary Club** for MVP.

MVP club count remains unchanged:

- **Science Club**
- **Literary Club**

This is a direct replacement, not an increase in scope. The MVP still has 2 clubs, 1 weekly 60-minute meeting per club, and 1 contained 90-minute special event per club.

## Rationale

The club list should support the strongest version of the MVP cast rather than force the cast to fit an earlier abstract club slate.

Science Club still fits the emerging cast through Amelia Price and the academic/project-pressure side of the game.

Literary Club fits Eleanor "Nell" Graves more cleanly than Drama Club because her strongest accepted concept centers on:

- private writing;
- anonymous or semi-hidden authorship;
- poems, short stories, fragments, and dark romantic writing;
- exposure, misreading, and reputation damage;
- fear of being emotionally visible again.

Drama Club remains a valid high-school fantasy, but it no longer has a strong MVP core-cast anchor. Keeping it would either require bending Nell toward performance/theatre or letting a non-core NPC carry the club special event. Both options weaken the MVP relationship/event integration.

## Zine Deadline Crisis

`Zine Deadline Crisis` is a 90-minute Literary Club special event where the club must finalize a small zine, school magazine page, reading board, or literary showcase material before a deadline.

The pressure should center on:

- authorship;
- consent;
- editing;
- layout;
- reputation;
- whether a private, anonymous, misattributed, or vulnerable piece risks becoming public.

The player can help through:

- editing;
- layout;
- practical errands;
- emotional support;
- public reading;
- protecting someone's boundaries around authorship.

Scope guardrails:

- no large publishing system;
- no large performance scene;
- no minigames required;
- text-forward choices and relationship flags are enough;
- one contained 90-minute event.

## Impact on Cast Design

Please continue MVP cast work assuming:

- Amelia can remain the Science Club anchor.
- Drama Club is deferred/full-game, not deleted from the overall fantasy.
- If a theatre/performance archetype appears later, it should not be required for MVP club coverage.
