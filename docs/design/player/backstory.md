# Player Character — Backstory & Setup

## Origin

The player character comes from a **small rural town** that has no high school.
To continue their education, they must leave home and move to **Lakeview** — a larger
town roughly an hour away — where they will live in a school dormitory for all three years.

This is not a choice they made eagerly. The opening Polish-language dialogue makes this clear:

> *"Ehhhh... Trzy kolejne lata bez starych."*
> ("Three more years without the old folks.")

They are not running away from home. They are being sent away by necessity, missing their
parents, and arriving in an unfamiliar town the day before school starts.

---

## Intro Arc

| Beat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Arrival** | Player is on a bus to Lakeview on August 29, arriving in the late afternoon |
| **First look** | Bus enters the town — first view of Lakeview from the road |
| **Finding the dorm** | Player navigates from the bus stop to the student dormitory |
| **Move-in** | Unpacks, sees the dorm room for the first time |
| **Eve of school** | Quiet evening before Day 1, sets the tone |

---

## Character Creation

At some point before or during the intro, the player defines:

- **Name** (first + last)
- **Gender**
- **Starting knowledge/skill levels** (per subject — see stat system)
- **Preferences** (hobbies, food) — affect interaction outcomes

---

## Dorm Setup

The player lives in the **Lakeview High student dormitory** for the full 3 years.
The dorm room is a persistent home base:

| Activity | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Sleep (22:00 enforced) | Ends the day, restores Energy |
| Study at desk | Knowledge gain, no classmate bonuses |
| Manage inventory / backpack | Prepare items for the next day |
| Receive NPC visits | Classmates can drop by in the evening (relationship gate) |
| Phone use | Messaging, social media, map — available any time in dorm |

---

## Notes

- Parents are back in the home village — they appear in holiday scenes and messages
- The player has no car; travel within Lakeview is on foot or by taxi (via smartphone map)
- The dorm has shared common areas (not yet fully designed)
